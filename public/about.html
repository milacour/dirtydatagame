Organisation

DirtyData is an organization dedicated to raising awareness about CO2 emissions caused by websites and promoting sustainable digital practices. We encourage everyone to participate in a collective effort to reduce CO2 emissions and preserve our planet for future generations.

Mission

Our mission is to help people understand the impact their online activities have on the environment and encourage action to reduce their carbon footprint. We believe that digital sustainability is an essential part of the overall effort to combat climate change.

By focusing on CO2 emissions related to web hosting, data servers, and internet traffic, we aim to inspire both individuals and businesses to make more environmentally friendly choices in their online behavior. DirtyData is working to raise awareness through research, education, and interactive games. We want to empower people and companies to make informed decisions and reduce their carbon footprint by choosing websites and digital services that are more eco-friendly.
