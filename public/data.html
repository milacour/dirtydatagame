Data

We have used SimilarWeb and Website Carbon to create a database of data about different websites. SimilarWeb has provided me with insights into web traffic for these websites, while Website Carbon has helped me estimate the environmental impact of each website in terms of CO2 emissions.

By using SimilarWeb, we have been able to gather information about the traffic on various websites. I have gained insights into the number of visitors, page views, average time spent on the website, and much more.

On the other hand, we have used Website Carbon to assess the environmental impact of each website. Using this tool, I have been able to estimate the CO2 emissions for each website based on factors such as server infrastructure, content size, and visit frequency. This has given us a better understanding of how different websites contribute to climate change.

By combining data from SimilarWeb and Website Carbon, we have been able to compare and evaluate different websites from both traffic and environmental perspectives. We have been able to identify websites with high traffic that also have a low environmental impact, and vice versa. This has helped us gain a deeper understanding of the relationship between website popularity and their environmental footprint.
